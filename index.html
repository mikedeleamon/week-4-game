Feed Pikachu



Pikachu is Hungry but he's only so hungry. Think of it as a game of blackjack. Each article of food fills the yellow mouse to a certain amount. Get the right amount and Pikachu will be full and happy. Give pikachu too much and he'll get sick and faint.
